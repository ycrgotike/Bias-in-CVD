Capabilities

What the system analyzes

🫀
Cardiac Biomarkers
Evaluates resting BP, cholesterol, fasting blood sugar, and max heart rate to build a complete cardiac profile.
📈
ECG Analysis
Incorporates resting ECG results and ST-segment depression (Oldpeak) to detect electrical abnormalities.
🤖
Multi-Model Selection
Trains 9 classifiers — from XGBoost to CatBoost — and automatically selects the best performing model.
⚡
Real-time Inference
Patient data is processed instantly through the trained pipeline with sub-second prediction response.
📊
Recall Optimized
Model selection prioritizes recall score to minimize false negatives — critical in medical screening.
🔬
Research Grade
Built on the UCI Heart Disease dataset merged from Cleveland, Hungary, Switzerland, and VA Long Beach.
